About the film

The First Step is a film about today, about the aspirations of a young man, and the effect of social media on his psyche. The film's structure is a reflection of his mental state. The story centered around the question, 'What would you do if you met yourself?'

Inspired by Christopher Nolan's 'Following', and the short stories of Satyajit Ray, 'The First Step' is a film designed to have a big impact within a small runtime.
